Twitter Flags Privacy Policy

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension displays country and regional flags next to Twitter usernames by reading publicly available location information from Twitter user profiles. Location data is cached temporarily in your browser's memory during your session only. The extension does not collect or store personal information, does not track your browsing activity, does not send data to external servers, does not use cookies or persistent storage, and does not modify your Twitter experience beyond displaying flags. All processing happens locally in your browser. Location information is retrieved from Twitter's public API using your existing Twitter session and is only used to display the appropriate flag icon.
